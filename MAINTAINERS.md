# Maintainers

Cyberful is owned and initially maintained by
[@ottaviofogliata](https://github.com/ottaviofogliata).

Repository ownership and review responsibility are encoded in
[`.github/CODEOWNERS`](.github/CODEOWNERS). Future maintainers may be added here
and to CODEOWNERS through a reviewed pull request. Security and conduct reports
are handled privately according to [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) and
[`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
