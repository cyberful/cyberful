# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Cyberful welcoming and harassment-free for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

Examples include demonstrating empathy, respecting differing viewpoints,
giving and accepting constructive feedback, taking responsibility for mistakes,
and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling,
insults, personal or political attacks, harassment, publishing private
information without permission, and other conduct inappropriate in a
professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening,
or harmful.

Report conduct concerns privately through
<https://github.com/cyberful/cyberful/security/advisories/new> and begin the
title with `Code of Conduct`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as
confidentially as practical. Maintainers who are the subject of a report will
not participate in its resolution.
